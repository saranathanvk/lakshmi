About Our Trust & Academy

Welcome to Lakshana Sri Educational Trust.

Lakshana Sri Educational Trust is a government-registered organization (Govt Reg.No BK4/5/2022, UDYAM - TN05 - 0040820) dedicated to providing free higher education guidance and counseling.

We empower students to make informed career choices, navigate complex admission processes, and excel in competitive exams, ensuring a brighter future for all.
